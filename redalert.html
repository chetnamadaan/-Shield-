Red alert Areas: Unsafe Locations for Women

Stay informed and aware of areas identified as high-risk for women.

The map below highlights locations that are known to be unsafe based on real-time data and reports from our users.

By visualizing these areas, you empower yourself to make informed decisions about your routes, ensuring you can avoid potentially dangerous situations.

These red-alert areas are updated frequently to help you plan your routes and stay safe.

We encourage you to stay vigilant and utilize our resources to enhance your safety.

Regularly check the map for updates and share this vital information with others to create a safer community for everyone.

The map given below will provide information about the red-alert areas.

Notable Red alert Areas

  • Area 1: Known for frequent reports of harassment at night.
  • Area 2: High rate of theft and mugging incidents.
  • Area 3: Poor lighting and isolated streets, with several reported attacks.
  • Area 4: Frequent catcalling and verbal harassment, especially around public transport.
