Red Alert Areas

Stay informed about high-risk areas in your city with our comprehensive red Alert map. We provide real-time data and updates on locations where incidents of harassment or violence have been reported. This information helps you plan your routes and make informed decisions about your safety.

Our platform collaborates with local authorities and community reports to update the red Alert areas regularly. By staying aware of these areas, you can take necessary precautions and avoid potentially dangerous situations.

Emergency Alert System


Stay Safe with Our Emergency Alert System

Our advanced Emergency Alert System ensures immediate help in critical situations. With features like:


  • * SOS Button: Instantly send an alert with your location to emergency contacts or authorities.
  • * Live Streaming: Share real-time video and audio with emergency contacts for immediate assistance.
  • * Automated Alerts: Smart threat detection sends distress signals automatically when danger is detected.

Empowered by GPS tracking and seamless integration with local emergency services, our system helps responders find you quickly. Stay safe with real-time alerts, automated check-ins, and safety reminders, ensuring you're always prepared.
