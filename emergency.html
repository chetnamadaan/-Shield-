How to Use the SOS Alert System

  1.   Open the app and press the SOS button.
  2.   Your location will be sent to emergency contacts.
  3.   Use live streaming to share your situation if necessary.
  4.   Stay in touch with emergency contacts via chat.
  5.   Activate the emergency siren for added attention.
  6.   Check the emergency contacts list to ensure it's updated.
  7.   Use the panic feature to alert nearby users.
  8.   Follow any instructions provided by emergency contacts.
  9.   Ensure your device's location services are enabled.
  10.   Stay calm and provide updates as needed.
